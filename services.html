Design Thinking Solutions

This is an iterative process, where we revisit each step only as much as is required, in an Agile manner, to help us focus on finding your needed solution as fast as possible.

EMPATHY

We work with you to explore and discover what challenges you are facing today. Together we learn how your systems operate, Research options, and Define what your core needs are.

Results

  • A clearly defined understanding of what your needs are, and some ways that might work to address things as we move into the CREATIVITY part of the cycle.
  • Designs and Definitions around what you are doing today, and possible directions going forward.

CREATIVITY

We work with your teams to explore and clarify way's to resolve your problem. During this time we Ideate on what is possible, while also Prototype possibilities only as much as is needed to help inform the next phase.

Results

  • We should be ready to Choose, starting the first step of RATIONALITY and building your solution.

RATIONALITY

The last phase of Design Thinking is to build. We first work with you to Choose the most powerful solution meeting your needs, then we Implement with your teams, followed by Learning as we look back and consider ways to improve our process.

Results

  • Your solution is operational at the end of this phase, and we are looking into other ways to help!
