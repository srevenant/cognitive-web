Design Thinking is a solution-focused process that models the efficient ways designers and architects work through some of the most challenging problems. This is different from problem-focused design, where a fix is decided prior to understanding the real problem.

User Experience Design (UX) and Design Thinking work together. UX is about understanding how you and your company interacts with the world around it, focusing on Empathy and understanding a person or user's needs.

Design Thinking is an iterative process, where we revisit each step only as much as is required to help us focus on finding your needed solution as fast as possible. It begins by Empathizing with your needs, followed by a cycle of Creativity where we work with your team to identify options, and concluding with Rationality as we help you choose, implement, and then learn.
